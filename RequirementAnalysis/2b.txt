Some common terminologies in the Internet Banking System:
- Internet Banking:
A service that allows an account holder to obtain account information and manage certain banking transactions through a personal computer via the financial institution's Web site on the Internet.
- Individual Account:
An account in the name of one individual.
- Interbank Network:
A computer network that enables ATM cards issued by a financial institution that is a member of the network to be used to perform ATM transactions through ATMs that belong to another member of the network.
- Bank Custodian:
A bank custodian is responsible for maintaining the safety of clients' assets held at one of the custodian's premises, a sub-custodian facility or an outside depository.
- Balance Transfer:
The process of moving an outstanding balance from one credit card to another.
